WHAT WE DO

AJ positively impacts the environment by collecting 300 Tons of clothing daily and classifying it in order to achieve sustainability.

Sustainable DistributionUsed clothing in all Fostering a circular economy, where the lifespan of garments is extended, promoting sustainability and responsible consumption.

WHY WE ARE HERE

Recycling and Up-cycling of used and excess clothes to reduce textile waste and its associated risks on nature, allowing us to achieve sustainability goals for a better future for our world.

WHAT TO ACHIEVE

We want to make it easier and faster for individuals and enterprises to contribute in recycling their extra clothes by integrating containers as a physical presence in commercial and social locations and providing vehicles to support our smart digital operations

Global Access
